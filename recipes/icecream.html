Home Made Ice Cream

This is how you do it

Boil the milk and put some sugar and sahlep dust to it

When its boiled put the chocolate in it and stir it till its melted

When it is in the right consistency turn of the heat and put some rom into it

And Woala. Put into freezer.

Tips

  1. Constantly stir while its boiling
  2. After put into freezer take it out every 30 min and stir
  3. Dont be shy on rom
